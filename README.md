# 📦 Supply Chain Performance & Risk Analysis

## 📌 Executive Summary
This project provides a data-driven analysis of supply chain operations, covering **$25.3M in total sales** and **504.3K units ordered**. The analysis focuses on evaluating supplier reliability, identifying logistical bottlenecks, and mitigating supply chain disruptions.

---

## 🚀 Strategic Insights (Data-Driven)

### 💰 Financial Performance
* **Top Category:** The **Electronics** category is the leading revenue driver, contributing **$5.69M** to total sales.
* **Order Volume:** Total quantity ordered reached **504.3K units** across all sectors.

### ⚠️ Risk & Reliability
* **Supplier Risk Profile:** **51.4% (514 orders)** of the total count are flagged as **"At Risk"**.
* **Reliability Metrics:** The average supplier reliability score is **0.7**, indicating a critical need for improved supplier vetting.
* **Disruption Drivers:** The primary causes of disruptions are **Shortages** and **Weather conditions**.

### 🕒 Operational Bottlenecks
* **General Delays:** The overall average delay is **1.9 days**.
* **Peak Delay:** A critical spike occurs in **December**, where delays jump to **4.2 days**.
* **Logistics Efficiency:** * **Air Shipping:** Most reliable mode with the lowest delays (approx **1.75 days**).
    * **Sea & Rail:** Experience the highest average delays (over **2.0 days**).

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack & Methodology
* **Tool:** Power BI / Data Visualization.
* **Key Metrics:** Order Value, Supplier Reliability Score, Supply Risk Flag, and Disruption Severity.
